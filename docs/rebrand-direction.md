# Pordee Rebrand Direction

This document records the new app name and logo direction that fits the current Pordee design direction in `../../TangMod/docs/design.md`.

The existing `TangMod / ตังค์หมด` name is playful and memorable, but it carries a negative "money is gone" tone. The new direction should keep the Thai-first warmth while moving the emotional promise toward balance, enoughness, and daily calm.

## Recommended Name

### `พอดี`

English support name: `Pordee`

Tagline: `เงินพอดี ชีวิตเบาขึ้น`

## Why This Name

- `พอดี` is short, Thai-first, and easy to say.
- It turns the product promise positive: not "money is gone", but "money feels just right".
- It fits a daily personal finance app better than a bank-like or investment-like name.
- It works with the new design direction: soft, calm, friendly, practical.
- It can hold expense tracking, income tracking, goals, and planning without sounding narrow.

## Brand Idea

`พอดี` is a friendly money companion that helps people make daily money decisions feel balanced enough for real life.

The brand should not promise wealth, optimization, or strict discipline. It should promise clarity, calm, and small repeatable habits.

### Wordmark

![Pordee wordmark](../source/rebrand-source/pordee-wordmark.svg)

The wordmark is Thai-first. `พอดี` should be the primary logo text, with `Pordee` as support text for URLs, app stores, and bilingual contexts.

### App Mark

![Pordee mark](../source/rebrand-source/pordee-mark.svg)

The mark uses a soft loop plus a small balance path:

- Coral loop: daily money flow, the main action energy.
- Teal path: balance, positive progress, and the "พอดี" feeling.
- Lime dot: tiny milestone or spark, never a dominant color.
- Sky background: calm, friendly, Thai-first app surface.

The original concept SVGs are kept as exploration material. The current final logo visual direction lives in `app/public/logo/pordee-pd-logo.png`.

## Mascot Concept

![Pordee mascot contact sheet v1](../source/rebrand-source/pordee-mascot-contact-sheet-v1.png)

The new mascot direction is a soft money-pouch / bean character. It keeps the friendly body language of the old TangMod mascot, but removes the negative "deflated / money is gone" tone.

### Mascot Asset Pack V1

![Pordee mascot states on sky](../source/rebrand-source/mascots/pordee-mascot-contact-sheet-sky.png)

Transparent trial assets live in `source/rebrand-source/mascots/`.

- `pordee-mascot-saving.png`
- `pordee-mascot-warning.png`
- `pordee-mascot-thinking.png`

These are usable for early UI trials. Before final production, refine state-to-state consistency, especially the top loop/knot on `normal` and `happy`.

### Mascot Anatomy

- White rounded body: calm, simple, and reusable on different UI backgrounds.
- Coral loop on top: connects directly to the `พอดี` logo mark and primary action color.
- Teal smile/path: visualizes balance and the feeling of "พอดี".
- Tiny lime moments: milestone or idea highlight only.
- Thin ink outline: keeps the mascot readable at small UI sizes.
- Soft sky shadow: grounds the character without heavy depth.

### Mascot States

- **Normal** - neutral helper for dashboard and empty states.
- **Happy** - success after adding a transaction or hitting a small goal.
- **Saving** - holds a lime coin-like milestone object for saving and goal progress.
- **Warning** - gentle concern for overspending, errors, or unusual spending.
- **Thinking** - helpful assistant, tips, and onboarding prompts.

The original contact sheet is a raster concept. The V1 split assets are transparent PNG trials, not final hand-cleaned production assets.

## Color Usage

- Wordmark primary: `#172026`
- Primary action and mark stroke: `#FF6B5A`
- Positive balance path: `#18A999`
- Background sky: `#EAF7FF`
- Highlight dot only: `#B7F34A`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`

Do not switch the logo to black-pill or banking blue. The new identity should stay close to the Pordee design system.

## Typography

Primary product font is `LINE Seed Sans`.

For the logo, start with LINE Seed Sans as the reference skeleton, then refine the final wordmark manually before app-store or trademark-grade production export. The current editable wordmark SVG candidates still use live text.

## Naming Alternatives Considered

### `เบาใจ`

Soft and emotional, but less explicitly about money. Better as an assistant feature name than the product name.

### `ตังค์ดี`

Close to `TangMod` and very easy to understand, but it is more generic and less ownable than `พอดี`.

### `ใช้ดี`

Friendly and habit-oriented, but it sounds like a product review phrase and is not specific enough for finance.

## Do

- Lead with the Thai name `พอดี`.
- Use `Pordee` for English support only.
- Keep the logo simple enough for app icon, favicon, and mobile header.
- Keep the mascot connected to the logo language, but separate from the logo itself.
- Keep coral, teal, sky, and tiny lime aligned with `../../TangMod/docs/design.md`.

## Don't

- Do not use wallet, piggy bank, chart, bank, or coin-stack symbols.
- Do not make the logo feel like an investment or fintech bank.
- Do not use The Money Things' black CTA as a brand signature.
- Do not make lime the main logo color.
- Do not make the mascot the logo.
