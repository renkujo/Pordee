# Pordee Rebrand Brief

## Name

Primary Thai name: `พอดี`

English support name: `Pordee`

Tagline: `เงินพอดี ชีวิตเบาขึ้น`

## Positioning

`พอดี` is a Thai-first personal finance app for people who want daily clarity without feeling judged by their money app.

The brand should feel friendly, calm, and practical. It should help users feel that money can be handled in small repeatable moments, not through strict discipline or wealth-maximizing pressure.

## Emotional Promise

Money does not need to feel perfect. It needs to feel understandable, balanced, and enough for real life.

## Visual Principles

- Thai-first, but readable in bilingual contexts.
- Soft, rounded, tactile, and warm.
- Coral is the action and brand-energy color.
- Teal is the balance and positive-progress color.
- Lime is a tiny milestone highlight only.
- Pale sky keeps the app calm and light.
- The mascot supports the product, but the logo remains the primary brand signature.

## Color Direction

| Token | Hex | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Coral | `#FF5C4D` | Primary logo stroke, main action energy |
| Teal | `#18A999` | Balance path, income/positive state |
| Lime | `#B7F34A` | Small milestone dot only |
| Charcoal | `#172026` | Primary text |
| Sky | `#EAF7FF` | Calm app background |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Surfaces and icon tile |

## Logo Direction

Use a `P + D` loop mark that feels like a soft glossy gel object:

- lowercase-style `P` loop on the left
- open `D` curve on the right
- teal smile/balance stroke underneath
- single lime dot near the lower right
- rounded ends and inflated 3D material

Avoid flat bank logos, wallet icons, piggy banks, coin stacks, charts, and strict finance symbolism.

## Mascot Direction

Use a soft money-pouch / bean companion character:

- friendly, rounded body
- coral loop detail
- teal smile/path detail
- tiny lime accents for milestones
- gentle expression states: normal, happy, saving, warning, thinking

The mascot should feel helpful, not childish or overly comic.
